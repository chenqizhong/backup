SDMS Policy for Hygiene
Safety and Environment Protection (HSE)

SDMS adheres to the responsibilities of HSE, and coordinates with global energy conservation, carbon reduction and national wind power development for sustainable business administration. We hereby promote the management systems of HSE, and use appropriate technology and management tools for reducing the risks from operations in terms of safety and hygiene, and implement activities to enhance the environmental protection. We also commit to：

  1. protect safety of labors and eliminate potential risks from work.
  2. implement hygiene management and promote labors’ health.
  3. induct green energy of wind power and protect environment & ecology.
  4. conform with HSE legislation and devote to pollution prevention.
  5. do our best to fulfill the customer's request and always be legal.
  6. involve by entire personnel in improvement and upgrading HSE performance.

Health and safety targets

The primary aim is to ensure that all operations performed and controlled by SDMS will be conducted in such a manner that Health and Safety protection standards are of the utmost importance throughout the duration of the project. The ultimate aim of the project is zero harm to all personnel involved in the works and those who may be affect by the work. Other main targets include:

  1. Accurate reporting of all accidents and incidents.
  2. Transparent reporting of all near misses and hazards observations.
  3. All person participated in this project in site receive appropriate training.
  4. All persons participated in this project in site will wear appropriate PPE.

Environmental Targets

The primary aim is the prevention of environmental pollution, ensuring that all operations performed and controlled by SDMS will be conducted in such a manner that environmental protection regulations are of the utmost importance throughout the duration of the project. The ultimate aim is the prevention of environmental pollution that our activities may cause. Other main target include:

  1. No environmental pollution fine by Environmental Protection Bureau.
