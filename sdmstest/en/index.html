About Us
SING DA MARINE STRUCTURE

In order to cooperate with the government on boosting the development of offshore wind power industry, and to support the government policy on localization of industries, China Steel Corporation (below as CSC) has invested and founded Sing Da Marine Structure Corporation (below as SDMS) on 19 April 2018. The capital of SDMS is NT$3.421 billion.
