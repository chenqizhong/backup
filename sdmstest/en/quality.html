Quality Policy

Sing Da Marine Structure Company actively promotes and improves quality management system by means of all member participating. We will spread dedication to quality to every aspect of the company and creat a culture of continuous innovation and improvement in order to leveling our capability in business marketing, project planning, manufacturing technique, production efficiency and product quality indication to international leading level.

We pursue not only assure customer satisfaction but also to be a trustworthy offshore wind power foundation supplier. In order to achieve this goal, we commit to:

  1. Master core manufacturing technology, maximize production capability, and coach & mentor employee to develop talent and achieve stronger performance.
  2. Implement supply chain management to ensure performance compliance, reliability, cost efficient to realize industry upgrading for global market competitiveness.
  3. Establish and implement a reliable Quality Management System enhancing business negotiation, project assessment, supply chain fitness and strengthen fit-up/ dimension control/ welding/ coating capability to ensure product quality and production capability.
  4. Perform contractual requirements on quality and on schedule, via adopting international technology resources, devoting innovation and continuous improvement, become a reliable and trustworthy off-shore wine turbine foundation global supplier.
