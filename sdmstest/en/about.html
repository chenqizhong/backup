Company Development History

In order to cooperate with the government on boosting the development of offshore wind power industry, and to support the government policy on localization of industries, China Steel Corporation (below as CSC) has invested and founded Sing Da Marine Structure Corporation (below as SDMS) on 19 April 2018 with the capital of NT$3.421 billion.

SDMS invests in building the offshore wind power jacket foundation production line in the Kaohsiung Marine Technology Industrial Innovation Zone, Singda Harbor. The size of the plant base is about 27 hectares. The investment has carried out our commitment to support the government policy on localization of offshore wind power foundation production.

Business

Sing Da Marine Structure estimates to complete its construction project of the plant by the end of 2019, and begin commissioning in January 2020. In the future, SDMS will focus on the production of subsea foundations.

Development

Sing Da Marine Structure is the first company in Taiwan to break ground on the jacket foundation factory. We introduce industrial technologies from experienced providers of European industrial services including Nervión Industries and DNV-GL, and cooperate with a supply chain of around 20 local companies. We will continue to devote ourselves in supporting the local offshore wind power industrial chain in the future, and meet the production and supply requirements of the offshore wind power foundations from the government. Furthermore, we will do our best to assist government agencies in promoting industrial upgrading, and promote the development of the local economy.
